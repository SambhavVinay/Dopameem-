Welcome to Dopameme!

Dopameme is a social platform where students can share posts and short videos called Dops. To maintain privacy, all accounts are private by default, and you must follow someone to see their posts and Dops.

Rules & Regulations

  • Respect all users. Harassment, bullying, or abusive content is prohibited.
  • Posts are pictures, and Dops are short videos – keep content appropriate.
  • Do not share someone else’s content without permission.
  • Follow others to view their posts; do not attempt to bypass privacy settings.
  • Report any inappropriate content to the moderators.

Technical Notes

Dopameme is a student project. The server may not always be stable, and performance might vary. Please be patient if things don’t work perfectly.
